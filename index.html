MOTORIDE

The Motorbike mod adds a powerful motorbike to the game, allowing players to navigate terrain at high speeds and explore the wilderness of Minecraft with a new level of excitement. So grab your helmet and get ready to hit the road!

MAKE IT WORK!

  • Enable experiments on in your game settings
  • Obtain the motorbike through one of the following methods:
  • Use the command "/give @p pa:Motorbike" in the chat box
  • Grab the motorbike egg from the creative inventory
  • Note that the motorbike is only available in creative mode and cannot be obtained in survival mode
  • Switch to creative mode before attempting to get the motorbike.
